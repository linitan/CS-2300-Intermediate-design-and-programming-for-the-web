My road trip to CA

Five persons, Half Month, From LA to SF
To my beloved friends

What is the trip?







In this winter holiday, Four of my friends and I took a 15-day road trip. We started at Las Vegas, through Death Valley National Park, San Deigo, Los Angeles, Santa Barbara, Monterey, Yosemite Park, to San Francisco.

This website is to introduce some fantasic places that we have explored to you. They are Los Angeles, San Deigo and Carmel. I want to show you the beautiful scenery and delicous foods there. If you want my trip plan or want to share your experience in those places, feel free to leave a message in the Feedback page.
